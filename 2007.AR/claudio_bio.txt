Claudio Castellini is a postdoctoral researcher at LIRA-Lab, University of Genova, Italy. After graduating in Electronic Engineerings in Genova in 1998, he obtained a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence in 2005 at the University of Edinburgh, Scotland, supervised by Dr. Alan Smaill. He currently works on machine learning, neuroscience and developmental robotics. He is involved in two European FET projects. His Ph.D. thesis has received the AIIA prize for best Italian dissertation in 2005.
