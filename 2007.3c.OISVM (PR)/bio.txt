Francesco Orabona
Francesco Orabona received the MS degree in Electronic Engineering in 2003 at the University of Napoli ``Federico II'', and the PhD degree at the University of Genova in 2007. He is currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Idiap Research Institute, Switzerland. His research interests include machine learning active vision in particular visual attention modeling, machine learning and information theory.

Claudio Castellini
Claudio Castellini is a postdoctoral researcher at LIRA-Lab, University of Genova, Italy. After graduating in Electronic Engineering in Genova in 1998, he obtained a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence in 2005 at the University of Edinburgh, Scotland, supervised by Dr. Alan Smaill. He currently works on machine learning, neuroscience and developmental robotics.

Barbara Caputo
Barbara Caputo is a senior researcher at Idiap since August 2006. She obtained her PhD in computer science from the Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm (2004). Her research interests are in the field of visual recognition for robot systems, online learning for cognitive systems and audio-visual recognition of unexpected events.

Luo Jie
Luo Jie received the BE degree in electrical engineering from Wuhan University, China, in 2005 and the MS degree in information technology from Royal Institute of Technology (KTH), Sweden in 2007. He is currently a PhD candidate at the Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lausanne, Switzerland. Since 2007, he also works as a research assistant at the Idiap Research Institute, Switzerland.

Giulio Sandini
Director of research at the Italian Institute of Technology and full professor of bioengineering at the University of Genoa. His main research interests are in the fields of computational and cognitive neuroscience and robotics. He graduated in Electronic Engineering at the University of Genova and was assistant professor at the Scuola Normale Superiore in Pisa until 1984.
